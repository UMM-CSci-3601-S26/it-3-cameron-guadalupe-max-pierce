GRADE REQUIREMENTS

This page lists the required items for each grade, provided by local schools.
Click on any item to modify its properties or remove it from the list.
Click the 'Add Item' button to add new items to the requirements. Alternatively, click on a Grade List to add items for a specific school and grade.
The lists can be sorted by various properties; by default, sorted by grade and school. Use the 'Sort By' section to change the sorting method.
To discard old lists, use the 'Reset Lists' button to clear all lists. (This CANNOT be undone!)
