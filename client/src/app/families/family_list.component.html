FAMILIES

This page lists families currently signed up to recieve supplies. Families can be entered manually, or added automatically when a survey is completed.
Click on any family to modify its properties or remove it from the list. Click the 'Add Family' button to add new families to the list.
The list can be sorted by various properties. Use the 'Sort By' section to change the order of families.
When resetting, use the 'Reset Families' button to clear the list. (This CANNOT be undone!)
